Sri Shanmugha college of engineering and technology

Sri Shanmugha College of Engineering and Technology is a private institute, established in 2011 in Salem, Tamil Nadu. Sri Shanmugha College of Engineering and Technology is approved by AICTE and offers a total of 11 courses at UG and PG levels in 9 specialisations such as Agriculture & Farm Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering and Cyber Security. Furthermore, it offer courses such as B.E. / B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech in Full Time mode. Sri Shanmugha College of Engineering and Technology fee structure includes a total tuition fee ranging from INR 50,000 to 2,00,000. Sri Shanmugha College of Engineering and Technology has an annual seat intake of 576 for all the programmes. It offers courses, in streams such as Engineering and IT & Software. As per the recent Sri Shanmugha College of Engineering and Technology placement records, the average median salary at the Institute is around INR 3,40,200. The institute has a safe and well-equipped campus with all the advanced facilities.
